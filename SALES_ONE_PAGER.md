# SALES ONE-PAGER: StackSpend Lead Qualification & Conversion

## The Elevator Pitch (15 seconds)

"StackSpend analyzes your AI tool spending and finds savings within 2 minutes. Most teams overspend by 20-40% on duplicate or underutilized tools. We help engineering teams cut costs without compromising quality."

---

## The Problem Statement

**Current Situation:**
- Teams use 4-6 AI tools concurrently (Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot, Gemini, OpenAI API)
- Each tool costs $20-40/month + fees
- Teams often have tool redundancy (paying for ChatGPT Team AND Claude Pro for the same use case)
- Seat oversubscription (paying for 12 Cursor seats when 4 are actively used)
- No visibility into spend or optimization opportunities

**Pain Points We Address:**
1. "We don't know how much we're spending on AI tools"
2. "We might be overpaying but don't know where to look"
3. "Our team uses different tools—should we consolidate?"
4. "API costs are unpredictable"

---

## The Solution

### Free Audit (2 minutes, no signup)
1. Tell us your team size & AI tool stack
2. StackSpend analyzes pricing & usage patterns
3. Get specific recommendations + savings estimate
4. Example: "You're paying $800/month but could spend $480/month with Cursor Pro instead of Cursor + GitHub Copilot"

### Premium Plan ($29/month for SMB)
- Monthly automated audits (pricing changes detected)
- Savings tracking dashboard
- Team member invites
- Seat usage monitoring
- Contract checklist

### Enterprise Plan ($500+/month)
- Custom audit frequency
- Admin console with policies
- Vendor negotiation support
- Integration with procurement tools

**Opening:** "Most engineering teams overspend 20-40% on redundant AI tools. Have you benchmarked your stack against what other teams are doing?"

**Follow-up:** "Would you be interested in a quick 2-minute audit to see if there's optimization potential?"

**Objection handling:** "It takes 10 minutes to add up subscriptions. We can do it in 2 minutes and show you exactly where the waste is."

### For Founders / CEOs:

**Opening:** "AI tool costs are now the 3rd biggest software budget item (after Slack and AWS). Are you optimizing for it?"

**Follow-up:** "Quick question: how many different AI tools does your team pay for?"

**If >1 tool:** "You might have $200-500/month in consolidation savings. Want a quick analysis?"

### For Finance / Operations:

**Opening:** "AI tool subscriptions are becoming a managed expense category, but most companies don't have visibility."

**Follow-up:** "We help finance teams track and optimize AI spending the same way they do for SaaS."

**Objection:** "How do you prevent teams from just adding tools back?" → "You manage with policy (like any SaaS spend), but the visibility itself drives behavior change."

## Objection Handling

### Objection: "We don't have time for this"
**Response:** "2 minutes. That's the whole audit. Even if you save $50/month, that's $600/year."

### Objection: "We already did a spreadsheet audit"
**Response:** "Spreadsheets get outdated monthly. Pricing changes, vendors release new tiers, teams add tools. We automate that for $29/month."

### Objection: "We have a procurement process, can't just switch tools"
**Response:** "This isn't about forcing switches. It's about knowing your options. The data is there; you just need to see it."

### Objection: "We're locked into annual contracts"
**Response:** "Even so, knowing your savings potential helps with next year's budget planning. Plus, some recommendations are for existing tools (different plans), not complete switches."

### Objection: "ChatGPT/Cursor is all we need"
**Response:** "Maybe. Let's verify. Some teams use just one tool. Others have 5-6. The audit tells you what YOUR team actually needs."
