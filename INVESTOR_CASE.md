# INVESTOR CASE: Why AI Spend Intelligence is a $500M+ Market

## Executive Summary

**The Opportunity:** AI tools spending is the fastest-growing operational expense for tech teams, with zero visibility or control infrastructure.

**The Product:** StackSpend is the financial control layer for AI-native teams—like Expensify for AI subscriptions.

**The Business Model:** Freemium audit → lead capture → premium SaaS ($29-99/team/month) → enterprise consulting.

**The Path:** 100 free audits → 1,000 free audits → 100 paid teams (Month 6) → 5,000 paid teams (Year 2) → $1-2M ARR.

---

## Part 1: Why This Market Exists Now

### The Problem: AI Spend is Out of Control

**Current State (May 2026):**
- ChatGPT: $20/month (Pro) × 50M users = $12B/year
- Cursor: $20/month × 1M estimated users = $240M/year
- Claude: $20/month × 2M estimated users = $480M/year
- OpenAI API: Billions in inference costs, mostly waste
- GitHub Copilot: $39/user/month × 500k business users = $234M/year

**Total addressable AI tool market: ~$20B+ annually**

### The Control Vacuum

Unlike SaaS spend management (Expensify, Vendr, Coupa):
- No visibility into tool stacks
- No policy enforcement
- No cost optimization tooling
- No procurement workflow
- No consolidation logic

**Result:** Teams are losing 20-40% to tool redundancy and overspend.

### Why Now?

1. **AI adoption is exploding** — Every team now has 3-5 AI tools (vs 0 three years ago)
2. **Subscription fatigue** — Teams are frustrated by constant tool costs
3. **Cost consciousness** — Economic pressure makes every $50/month count
4. **Standardization** — Pricing is consolidating (ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor are the "big 3")

**Acquisition:**

- Convert 5-10% of leads to paid
- Target: 100 paid teams by Month 6

### Phase 3: Enterprise + Consulting (Month 7-12)

**Goal:** Build high-touch revenue stream

**Enterprise Plan ($500/month+):**
- Unlimited audits, custom teams
- Admin console with governance policies
- Vendor negotiation support

**Consulting Services ($5k-20k per engagement):**
- AI tool procurement reviews

**Example:** "Your team is overspending by $50k/year on AI tools. We'll negotiate with your vendors and save you $30k. Fee: $5k" → 6-month payback.
